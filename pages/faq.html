Can I start if I don't have previous experience??

Of course, everyone who trains at our facility had their start at some point. Once you are training, always try to have one of our professionals nearby to assist you with any questions.

Can I get the money back once paid?

Once the month or any of the separate classes have been paid, you have a period of 5 days to repent, we also take into account people who have suffered from an illness that makes it impossible for them to attend the time captured, in which they must have proof of illness to verify this situation. If all those points are covered, we will refund 100% of your subscription.

I lost my belongings, can I get them back?

Our facilities are equipped with security cameras that are filming all the time, if we receive any notification or suspicious abduction we will contact you immediately. Having said that, the gym is not responsible for lost belongings.

If I train more than 5 times a week, can something happen to me?

Have you been training for 5 years or are you just starting? When you come back from the gym do you feel exhausted in the days after? Are you eating properly, maintaining a structured balance between your main macronutrients (protein, carbohydrates and fats)? don't you train to replenish those forces and come back stronger than ever to the gym? If all the answers to these questions are yes, then there is no harm in training 5 times a week.
